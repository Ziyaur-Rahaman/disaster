Derna Dam Collapse

In September 2023, the Derna dam in Libya collapsed following heavy rainfall from Storm Daniel. The failure released an estimated 30 million cubic meters of water, causing catastrophic flooding downstream. The city of Derna was partially destroyed, with casualty estimates ranging from 5,300 to 20,000 people. This disaster is the second-deadliest dam failure in history..

Cyclone Chido in Mayotte

In December 2024, Cyclone Chido struck the French overseas department of Mayotte, causing significant destruction. Satellite images revealed extensive damage across the region. The cyclone led to severe flooding and infrastructure collapse, displacing thousands of residents. Recovery efforts are ongoing as the community rebuilds from this devastating event.

Libya Floods

In September 2023, Storm Daniel caused catastrophic flooding in eastern Libya, particularly in the city of Derna. The collapse of two dams released massive amounts of water, leading to widespread destruction. Estimates of casualties range from 5,300 to 20,000 people. This event is considered the second-deadliest dam failure in history.

Our Services

Disaster Prediction

Disaster prediction involves the use of technology, data analysis, and scientific modeling to forecast potential natural or man-made calamities. It leverages tools like weather satellites, seismic monitoring, and AI-based algorithms to analyze patterns and predict events such as hurricanes, earthquakes, and floods.

Victim Assistance

Victim assistance focuses on providing immediate and long-term support to individuals and communities affected by disasters. Key elements include emergency relief, such as food, water, shelter, and medical care, to address immediate needs. Psychological support and counseling help victims cope with trauma and rebuild their lives.

Help Line

Help lines are critical communication channels providing immediate assistance during emergencies or disasters. They offer information, guidance, and emotional support to affected individuals and communities. Dedicated toll-free numbers connect victims with rescue services, medical aid, and essential resources.

About disaster

disaster is a severe and often unexpected event that leads to widespread harm, disruption, and distress. It can be categorized into natural disasters (earthquakes, tsunamis, floods) and human-made disasters (nuclear accidents, oil spills, industrial explosions). Disasters usually result in loss of life, infrastructure damage, and environmental degradation.
